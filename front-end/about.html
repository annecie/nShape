Our Mission

Physical inactivity in the U.S. is staggering. According to Healthy People 2020, approximately 36 percent of adults do not engage in any leisure-time physical activity.The purpose of this fitness app is to provide users with instructions and examples of one or more types of exercise, physical activity, nutritional programs, and other fitness topics to help them stay fit and lead a more active lifestyle.
